Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our custom shoes, ordering process, and more.

Customization

How does the customization process work?

Our customization process is simple and intuitive. Start by selecting your base style, then customize various elements including materials, colors, and sole type. You can even add custom artwork to make your shoes truly unique. Our 3D preview updates in real-time so you can see exactly how your custom shoes will look.

Can I upload my own artwork?

Yes! You can upload your own artwork in JPG, PNG, or SVG format (max 5MB). We'll print your design on the shoes according to your chosen placement. Please ensure you have the rights to use any artwork you upload.

Ordering

How long does it take to receive my custom shoes?

Custom shoes typically take 2-3 weeks to manufacture and ship. This includes time for crafting your unique design, quality checks, and shipping. You'll receive regular updates on your order status throughout the process.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and Apple Pay. All payments are processed securely through our encrypted payment system.

Shipping

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship worldwide! Shipping costs and delivery times vary by location. You can see exact shipping costs during checkout after entering your delivery address.

How can I track my order?

Once your order ships, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can use this number to track your package's journey through our shipping partners' websites.

Returns

What is your return policy?

We want you to be completely satisfied with your custom shoes. If they don't meet your expectations, you can return them within 30 days of delivery. Please note that due to the custom nature of our products, we can only accept returns for quality issues or manufacturing defects.

How do I initiate a return?

To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team through the Contact page. We'll provide you with a return shipping label and detailed instructions for the return process.

Still Have Questions?

Our customer service team is here to help you with any questions or concerns.
