Frequently Asked Questions

How do I check my application status?

You can check your application status by logging into your student portal and navigating to the "Application" section. Your current status, along with any pending requirements, will be displayed there. If you have specific questions about your application status, you can also contact the admissions office directly.

What documents are required for admission?

The required documents for admission include:

  • Completed application form
  • High school transcripts (10th and 12th grade)
  • Standardized test scores (if applicable)
  • Passport-sized photographs
  • Proof of identity (Aadhar Card, PAN Card, or Passport)
  • Recommendation letters (for specific programs)
How can I pay my tuition fees?

Tuition fees can be paid through multiple methods:

  • Online payment through the student portal
  • Bank transfer to the university account
  • Demand Draft in favor of "ITM Skills University"
  • Cash payment at the university finance office

For details regarding installment options or scholarship deductions, please contact the finance department.

What scholarship opportunities are available?

ITM Skills University offers various scholarships including:

  • Merit-based scholarships (based on academic performance)
  • Need-based financial aid
  • Sports excellence scholarships
  • Special category scholarships for women in STEM fields
  • Corporate-sponsored scholarships

The scholarship application window typically opens twice a year. For detailed eligibility criteria and application process, visit the Scholarships section under Financial Aid.

How do I access online classes?

Online classes can be accessed through the university's learning management system (LMS). Simply log in with your student credentials, and navigate to the "Courses" section. Each enrolled course will have virtual classroom links for scheduled sessions.

For technical issues with the LMS, please contact our IT support desk at it.support@itmskills.edu or call the helpline at +91-123-456-7890.
